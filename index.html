Ian A. Pshea-Smith

Spatial Epidemiologist & Vector Ecologist

I am a PhD candidate in Medical Geography and Global Health at the University of Florida, where I investigate how environmental conditions shape vector-borne disease transmission patterns for higher-risk groups. My research integrates geography, community ecology, and public health to understand how complex factors like climate change influence mosquito and tick communities.
